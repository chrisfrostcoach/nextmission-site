NET-02 / THE RITUALS

The battle rhythm of the net

Named rituals on a fixed cadence. You always know what's next and what's expected.

Daily
The net is open. Post the win or the wobble. Either gets answered — that's the point of a net.
Weekly
Orders Group posting. SITREPs land in the section channels; the week's brief goes up for all-call.
Fortnightly
Live call over Zoom — two a month. AARs and obstacles worked in the open. Led, not lectured.
Quarterly
Intake. A new class falls in and takes its numbers on the roll.

STANDARD — THE ROLL

Every member takes a number, issued in order of joining and held for as long as you're on the net. Founding members — the first intake, Class 26-08 — are marked as founding on the roll, permanently.
