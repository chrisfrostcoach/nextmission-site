FM-03 / SPECIMEN PAGE

Read a page before you buy it

EXCERPT — CH01 / HEALTH

The aim here is not the beach. It is operational readiness: steady energy through the day, a body that does what you tell it, sleep that actually repairs, and the ability to change your own state on demand instead of being run by it.

CH01.D2 — Cold Finish · Task
End your daily shower with 2 minutes of fully cold water. No easing in, no cutting it short.
Condition
Ordinary shower. Timer running where you can see it.
Standard
10 consecutive days logged. The rep counts when the timer hits 2:00, not when it feels like it.

Be clear about what this drill is. It will not fix anything medical and it is not magic. Its value is that it is unpleasant, voluntary, brief, and scheduled: ten straight days of doing an uncomfortable thing on command, when nobody would know if you skipped it. That is discomfort training. The transfer — to hard phone calls, hard conversations, hard mornings — is the point.
